Prevention measures :

To prevent the development of proliferative diabetic retinopathy, it is crucial to effectively manage diabetes by controlling blood sugar and blood pressure levels. Additionally, regular eye exams are essential to detect and monitor the condition. If needed, your eye doctor may recommend treatment options such as laser therapy or injections to prevent the condition from worsening.
